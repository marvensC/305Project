How to Use StudySync

Create An Account by entering your email and creating a password.
A verification email will be sent to your email. The account must be
verified to login.

Once You are logged in , you can customize your profile.
You can add a profile image.
Create a name a name and write your bio. An input bar will
show up and to save your answer make sure to hit the save button!

Now you can ready other peers questions and create a post of your own!
Navigate using the navigator by pressing the button on the top left corner.
Hit the forum hit. You can click on post to read them or create a post of
your own by pressing the "Create Post" button.

Now you can communicate with your peers. Use the navigator
and click on the chat tab. If you have a roomID you can join a
room. You can get help with your school work or help other students
with thier work. If you don't have a room key press the create room button and
send the code to your peers!

Keep your day organized. Create a list of all your daily task.
Press the "Add Task" button and type in the task that you want
to complete. Repeat this for the amount of task you have in a
day and conquer your day!!

Rate the APP. If you did or didn't enjoy the app let the
developers know your idea! In the setting tab you can reset your account ,
delete your account, or sign out.
